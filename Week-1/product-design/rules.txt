1. In school timings(7am - 9am), the speed limit will be 20km/hr.
2. Parking of vehicles on the road at the time of heavy traffic will be punishable.
3. From 11pm - 6am, all traffic lights will be disabled as the traffic is very low.
4. The time difference between traffic lights can be increased for differently abled people.
5. The timings for red light will be calculated according to the amount of traffic.
6. If the rate of traffic in one direction is more than a particular limit (which will be set in the system) , we declare that particular road to be one way until the traffic becomes light.
